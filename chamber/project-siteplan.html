Site Plan - Global Jobs Placements

Site Name:

The name of the website will be "GLOBAL JOBS PLACEMENTS". This is to ensure that everyone relates to it and the need to find jobs in Ghana is met.

Site Purpose:

The objective of the website is to connect job seekers in Ghana with relevant remote job opportunities globally. It aims to provide a user-friendly platform for employers to post job vacancies and for job seekers to find these opportunities easily.

Scenarios:

  • Job Seekers can create profiles, upload resumes, and apply for jobs.
  • Employers can post job listings and review applications.
  • Users can search and filter job listings based on various criteria.
  • Employers can trend job postings based on popularity and applications received.

Color Schema:

The proposed color scheme includes:

  • Primary Color: #007bff (Blue) - for headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #f8f9fa (Light Gray) - for background and sections.
  • Accent Color: #28a745 (Green) - for success messages and highlights.
  • Text Color: #343a40 (Dark Gray) - for body text.
  • Link Color: #6f42c1 (Purple) - for hyperlinks.

Typography:

The typography will follow the Google Fonts standard for accessibility:

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif - for a clean, modern look.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - for good readability.

Wireframe:

Wireframe will include:

  • Header section with logo and navigation.
  • Main content area featuring job listings, filters, and search bar.
  • Footer with contact information and social media links.

Footer:

Include copyright information, links to privacy policy, and terms of service.
